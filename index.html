Vinay Joshy

Statistics, University of Guelph

I’m a Master of Science in Statistics student at the University of Guelph supervised by the wonderful Dr. Zeny Feng. My research is on finite mixture models, particularly developing regularization methods for generalized linear regression mixture models. I completed my undergraduate degree in Bachelor of Mathematics at the University of Guelph and a Technical Diploma in Biotechnology at Mohawk College. My most recent CV can be found here.
